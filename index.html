Objective:

Move all 4 colored pieces clockwise around the board and into the corresponding home position in the center.

How to Play:

On your turn, click the die. Move any piece exactly that number of spaces. Move by clicking the space you want to move to, or the piece you want to move. If you land on another player's piece, it will be bumped back to starting position. You cannot bump yourself. If no move is possible, your turn will be skipped.
